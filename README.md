# 📈 hermes-health-apollo - Manage health data and daily schedules

[![Download Software](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-Hermes-blue)](https://github.com/vsj4394/hermes-health-apollo/releases)

Hermes Health Apollo links your wearable health devices and calendar data to your computer. It focuses on privacy by keeping your data on your local machine. This tool integrates Oura ring data with Google Calendar to suggest times for work and rest.

## ⚙️ System Requirements

This application works on Windows 10 and Windows 11. Ensure your computer has at least 4GB of RAM. You need a stable internet connection for the initial setup. You must have a Google account to sync your calendar. Keep your wearable device login details ready to connect your health data.

## 🚀 Setting Up Your Data

Launch the application to begin the configuration process. The initial dashboard asks you to connect your accounts. 

### Connect Google Calendar
Select the Calendar tab in the sidebar. Click the blue button labeled Link Google Account. A web browser window will open. Sign in to your Google account and grant the app permission to read your calendar events. This allows the app to see your daily schedule.

### Connect Wearable Data
Select the Wearables tab. Choose your device from the list. Follow the instructions to log in to your Oura account. The application pulls your sleep, activity, and readiness scores. This process happens in the background.

## 📊 Using the Application

The interface shows three main sections: Today, Insights, and Settings.

### Today View
The Today view pulls information from your calendar and your wearable device. It lists your meetings for the day. It also shows your readiness score from your Oura ring. If your readiness score remains low, the app suggests breaks between meetings.

### Insights Tab
View your long-term health trends here. The app displays charts for your sleep duration and activity levels over the past month. Use the filter buttons to change the date range.

### Settings
Adjust your alert preferences in the Settings menu. You can choose when the app notifies you about meetings. You can also export your data as a CSV file to share with health professionals.

## 🔒 Privacy and Local Storage

This application follows a local-first philosophy. Your health data stays on your local hardware. The application only sends data to Google to fetch your calendar events. It stores all local logs in an encrypted format on your hard drive. No health data goes to our servers.

## 🛠 Troubleshooting Common Issues

If the app fails to open, restart your computer then try again. If your wearable data does not refresh, navigate to the Wearables tab and click the Refresh button. Ensure your wearable device has a recent sync with its official mobile app. 

Check for software updates by clicking the Help menu and selecting Check for Updates. We deploy improvements regularly to maintain stability. If an error persists, close the application completely and relaunch it. Most issues resolve after a simple restart.

## 💡 Frequently Asked Questions

**Does the app work without an internet connection?**
Yes, once you sync your data, you can view your calendar and health stats offline. You need the internet to sync new data from your wearable or Google Calendar.

**Can I use multiple wearable devices?**
Currently, the software supports Oura devices. We plan to add more brands in future releases. 

**Is my data shared with other companies?**
No. Your data stays on your computer. We do not sell or share your personal health information with third parties.

**How do I uninstall the software?**
Open the Windows Control Panel. Select Programs and Features. Find hermes-health-apollo in the list and click Uninstall. This removes the application and its local data cache.

**Does this app track my location?**
No. This app focuses on health and calendar integration. It does not track your location.
